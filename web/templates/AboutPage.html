About {{.Header.AppName}}

What is {{.Header.AppName}}?

{{.Header.AppName}} is a turn-based strategy game inspired by classic hex-based wargames. Command your forces across varied terrain, capture bases, and outmaneuver your opponents in tactical battles.

Features

  • Turn-based combat - Plan your moves carefully and execute your strategy
  • Multiple unit types - Infantry, tanks, aircraft, naval vessels, and more
  • Terrain matters - Use mountains for defense, roads for speed, and bases for production
  • Multiplayer support - Challenge friends or play solo
  • Custom maps - Create and share your own battlefields

How to Play

Each turn, you can move your units, attack enemies, capture neutral or enemy bases, and build new units at bases you control. The goal is to eliminate all enemy units or capture their headquarters.

New to the game? Start by exploring the available maps or jump into a game.
